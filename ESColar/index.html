ESColar

EARTHQUAKE NAVIGATION APPLICATION
for PUP students, faculty, staff, and visitors.

how to use:

1CLICK Navigate (to head to the navigation page)

2SELECT Origin Point (where you are currently located)

3CLICK Start Navigation

4WAIT for ESColar to route the safest and shortest evacuation route.

5FOLLOW the displayed evacuation route by ESColar.

in case of emergency:

1CLICK Emergency (to head to the emergency page)

2CHOOSE a service to send your message to (location will automatically be sent upon confirmation)

for information:

1CLICK Information (to head to the information page)

2SELECT if you want information for what to do BEFORE, DURING, or AFTER an earthquake.

You may also SELECT Hotlines to see important lines that can be helpful for earthquakes.
