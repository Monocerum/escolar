INFORMATION

KNOW THE HAZARDS.

Familiarize yourself with earthquake hazards in your area.

KNOW THE EVACUATION AREA.

Identify the evacuation point of your area and determine the optimal evacuation routes.

PREPARE YOUR HOUSE/WORKPLACE.

Prepare evacuation plans; practice drop, cover, and hold on; practice earthquake safety procedures periodically.

PREPARE EMERGENCY KIT.

Make sure that it is accessible at all times and contains all the necessary things for an earthquake.

LEARN TO USE EQUIPMENT.

Learn to use the first aid kit, fire extinguishers, alarms, switching off waterlines, gas tanks, and circuit breaker.

PARTICIPATE DURING DRILLS.

Regularly practice the evacuation procedure.
